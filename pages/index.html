about us

Mc Larty Shipping Agents (Mc Larty) is an independent, privately owned shipping agency, founded in 2014, with extensive expertise and regional footprint. Our Management has between 15 and 35 years of experience in the shipping business, having worked at both sides of the desk, for top Shipping Lines as well as for main Shipping Agents, which results in a very close relationship with local authorities, port terminals, service providers and clients, of course. Our Operations Department has an extensive experience of working with major corporates, national shipping companies, private clients, grain houses and oil companies, among others, providing quality service across all shipping sectors.
